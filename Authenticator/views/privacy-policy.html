Privacy Policy

If you use the SevenZipBackup Plugin, you agree to this privacy policy.

Backup Files:

SevenZipBackup is a Minecraft plugin which assists the user in the creation and upload of server backup files to external cloud services. These files are directly uploaded to such services therefore SevenZipBackup will not and cannot collect any data regarding your server files. Any backup created is your property and not available to anyone else. This plugin does not access any sensitive data in your Google Drive, OneDrive and/or Dropbox that is not related to backing up your Minecraft world backups.

External Services:

SevenZipBackup may use external services such as the Google Drive API. If you choose to use these services, then Google's or any other service's privacy policies and terms of service will take effect. All credentials for such services will be stored locally on your machine.

Metrics:

This plugin uses bstats.org to collect metrics, such as the current count of servers using SevenZipBackup. You can disable it in your bstats config file.
